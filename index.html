About our Campuses

Don Honorio Ventura State University (DHVSU), located in Bacolor, Pampanga, Philippines, is a prominent state university with a rich history dating back to its founding in 1861. Initially established as a primary school by the Spanish friar, Don Felino Gil, it has evolved into a significant institution of higher learning in the region.
Campus and Facilities Main Campus: The main campus is situated in Bacolor and boasts various academic buildings, administrative offices, laboratories, and libraries. It also includes facilities for athletics, such as sports fields and gymnasiums, supporting a holistic educational environment.
Satellite Campuses: DHVSU has expanded to include satellite campuses in other areas, such as Mexico, Sto. Tomas, Porac, Lubao, and Apalit each providing localized access to the university's academic programs and resources.
Academic Facilities: The university is equipped with modern classrooms, computer laboratories, engineering workshops, and science laboratories. These facilities are designed to support the practical and theoretical education of students across various disciplines.
Academic Programs DHVSU offers a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs across different fields of study. These include:
Engineering and Architecture: Programs like Civil Engineering, Electrical Engineering, and Architecture are highly regarded. Education: Various education degrees cater to future teachers and educators. Information Technology: Courses in IT and computer science. Business and Management: Degrees in Business Administration, Accountancy, and Hospitality Management. Health Sciences: Programs in Nursing and Medical Technology. Community Engagement and Research The university emphasizes community involvement and research. It undertakes various outreach programs aimed at contributing to local development and provides students with opportunities to apply their knowledge in real-world settings.
Accreditation and Recognition DHVSU is recognized by the Commission on Higher Education (CHED) of the Philippines and has received various accreditations for its programs, ensuring quality education and adherence to national academic standards.
Student Life Student life at DHVSU is vibrant, with numerous organizations, clubs, and societies catering to diverse interests. These activities foster personal growth, leadership skills, and community spirit among students.
Overall, Don Honorio Ventura State University is a key educational institution in Central Luzon, known for its commitment to providing accessible and quality education to its students.
